Who We Are

NSS NIT Kurukshetra consists of highly motivated and dedicated members striving to bring peace and harmony. We work on a diverse range of social issues including blood donation, education, and environment by forming a consortium with several NGOs. "Not Me But You" is the motto that fuels our selfless service.

Core Objectives

Community Service

Engaging youth to understand the community and identify their needs and problems.

Leadership Development

Acquiring leadership qualities, democratic attitudes, and the capacity to meet emergencies.

Social Awareness

Developing a sense of social and civic responsibility while utilizing knowledge to find practical solutions.

Recent Achievements

State Level Best NSS Unit

Recognized for outstanding contribution to rural education integration.

Mega Blood Donation Drive

Successfully collected over 500+ units in a single day.

About Our Team

“The pen is mightier than the sword.” - Edward George Bulwer-Lytton Changing people’s mindset towards a more productive future by planting the seeds of humanity in every living soul, the content writers implement their creative and well-honed writing skills to provoke very ounce of humankind service. Accustomed to the immense power of words, we do our best at working for the needy community and make them feel the happiness of unity.

“Artist works deeply to make the art fine, That fine art then becomes a design.” Our design team deals with the task of designing posters for different social media handles, and various campaigns. Utilising talent of skilled designers, the team design posters that provoke a sensation among people for contributing towards a noble cause. Making posters for main festivals of our country is also a job for the team.

Web Designers team is accountable for designing the webpage and making the required improvements in it. This team provides users to access the website on any device for getting the detailed information about NSS in NIT KURUKSHETRA. This helps people to reach us easily and know more about us. They also upload about every main festivals of India.

“Bright sunshine comes every morning, Conveys the message that Mother Earth is mourning, Consciously we enjoy our entertainment, But the consequences are endured by the environment.” Focussed on saving the breath-givers, the environment team has the responsibility to convey a strong message about the vital importance of plants in our lives and how adverse the effects can be if we don’t take a stand against mandatory environmental problems like deforestation, global warming, pollution etc.

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.” - Nelson Mandela Emphasizing on providing education to every child and help them grow and develop in future, the education team takes up the job of teaching children and conducting recreational activities leading to an establishment of a foundation which will bear the fruit of building their intellectual skills.

Posting updated news and editing the interface of social media platforms, the social media team covers every detail of various activities organised and uploads on social media so that people can get updated of any important news. Celebrating every festival as one’s own, the team members post every brief detail about the festival which helps us to gain audience’s trust nationwide.

“It is health that is real wealth and not pieces of gold and silver.” - Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi Physical as well as mental fitness is a crucial element of our vitality. Acknowledging this fact, the sports team handles the matters related to sports affairs. In addition to giving knowledge about health, the team conducts numerous exercises such as Yoga,Meditation,Running etc. Objective is to keep everyone out of harm’s way.
